Problem Statement

Why adaptive hearing protection matters

Prolonged exposure to high audio levels through wireless earbuds can cause hearing fatigue, tinnitus, and permanent damage. Most systems only show warnings. HearWell goes further by intelligently enforcing protective sound limits, tracking earbud listening health over time, and reacting when Bluetooth-connected listening becomes unsafe.

Risk

Unsafe sound spikes pass through conventional earbuds with no intervention.

AI Response

HearWell predicts unsafe exposure and triggers a protective reduction before risk persists.

Outcome

Safer listening, lower exposure time, and healthier long-term hearing habits.
